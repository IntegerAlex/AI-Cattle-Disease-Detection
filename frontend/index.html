AI Cattle Disease Assistant

Helping farmers and veterinarians care for cattle

How It Works

1. Monitor Cattle Health

Farmers use a smartphone app with smart neckbands that track:

  • Oxygen levels
  • Heart rate
  • Activity levels

2. Check for Illness

Take photos of symptoms like:

  • Skin bumps (Lumpy Skin Disease)
  • Eye infections
  • Mouth blisters (Foot-and-Mouth Disease)

3. Get AI Analysis

The system analyzes:

  • Symptom photos
  • Vital signs from neckbands
  • Treatment history

to identify potential health issues.

4. Veterinary Support

Vets receive detailed reports and can:

  • Review AI suggestions
  • Send treatment plans
  • Contact farmers directly
